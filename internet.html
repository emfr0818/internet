What is the internet?

The internet is a worldwide network that is made up of many protocols. These specific protocols all communicate with eachother to solve different problems. Some examples of these problems might be having one connection network down and having to resort to a different one to communicate information.Without the internet it would be hard to imagine what our world would look like today, there would be no texting, no sending images at extreme speeds, and overall less identity theft.
